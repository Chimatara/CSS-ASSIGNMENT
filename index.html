What are the differences between Padding, Margin and border?

Hang on! we will be discussing it here

Margin Border Padding
Margin is the outer space surrounding a defined border. They define how an element interacts with adjacent elements or the edge of a screen Border is the perimeter of a box. It can be invisible or there could be a colored line Paddings are the inner space between the content and the border of your box
